<p><p>Noise-reducing acoustic vents are categorized into three main market types based on their installation location: window vents, wall vents, and other vents. Window vents are designed to be installed in windows and provide a barrier against external noise while allowing air circulation. Wall vents are installed directly into the walls and offer similar noise reduction benefits. The "other" market category includes vents installed in different locations such as doors or ceilings. These vents also aim to minimize noise infiltration while maintaining ventilation in various indoor environments.</p></p>
