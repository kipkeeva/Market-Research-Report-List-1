<p><p>The AMC CPU Card market consists of two main types: AdvancedTCA and MicroTCA. AdvancedTCA (ATCA) is targeted towards high-performance systems, typically found in telecommunications and data centers, supporting multiple processors and hot-swappable modules. MicroTCA, on the other hand, is designed for smaller form factors and less demanding applications, such as industrial control and embedded computing, offering lower power consumption and cost. Both market types provide standardized CPU cards that offer flexibility, scalability, and compatibility with various communication protocols for efficient data processing in their respective industries.</p></p>
