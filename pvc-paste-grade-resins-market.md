<p><p>PVC paste grade resins are widely used in several industries, including construction, automotive, and packaging. Two common methods for producing these resins are the micro suspension method and the emulsion method. In the micro suspension method, the PVC is suspended in a liquid medium, and a polymerization process is carried out to form the resin particles. On the other hand, the emulsion method involves dispersing PVC particles in water and carrying out a polymerization reaction. Both methods produce high-quality PVC paste grade resins suitable for various applications.</p></p>
