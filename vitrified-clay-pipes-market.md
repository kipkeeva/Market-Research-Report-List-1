<p><p>Vitrified Clay Pipes are a type of sewer pipe made from clay that has been subjected to high temperatures, resulting in a strong and durable material. The market for Vitrified Clay Pipes includes two main types: Open Trench Socket Pipes and Microtunneling (Jacking) Vitrified Clay Pipes. Open Trench Socket Pipes are installed using traditional excavation methods, where a trench is dug and the pipes are laid in place. On the other hand, Microtunneling (Jacking) Vitrified Clay Pipes are installed using a jacking technique, where the pipes are pushed underground without the need for extensive excavation.</p></p>
