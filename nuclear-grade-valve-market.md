<p><p>The nuclear grade valve market consists of various types of valves, each serving a specific purpose in nuclear power plants. Gate valves control the flow of fluid by using a sliding gate mechanism. Globe valves regulate fluid flow with a movable disk that covers or uncovers the flow path. Butterfly valves have a rotating disk that controls the flow by pivoting perpendicular to the direction of fluid. Ball valves utilize a spherical closure to regulate the flow. Check valves allow flow in one direction only. Diaphragm valves use a flexible diaphragm to control flow. Lastly, there are other types of valves designed for specific applications in nuclear power plants.</p></p>
