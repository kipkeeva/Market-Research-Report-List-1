<p><p>The Patient Lift Pendant (PLP) market comprises two main types: the single-mast and twin-mast markets. Single-mast PLPs are devices that use a single main pole for lifting and transferring patients. They are typically smaller and more maneuverable, best suited for homecare settings. On the other hand, twin-mast PLPs use two parallel poles that offer greater stability and weight capacity, making them ideal for hospitals and long-term care facilities. Both types play a crucial role in assisting healthcare professionals in safely lifting and transporting patients.</p></p>
