<p><p>Solar paste is a crucial component in the manufacturing of solar cells. There are two main types of solar paste: Ag paste and Al paste. Ag paste, also known as silver paste, is widely used due to its excellent conductivity in solar cell connections. It helps improve the efficiency of solar cells by enhancing electrical conductivity. On the other hand, Al paste, or aluminum paste, is utilized for rear-side aluminum contacts in solar cells. It assists in achieving better electrical contact for more efficient energy conversion. Both Ag paste and Al paste contribute to the overall performance and effectiveness of solar cells.</p></p>
