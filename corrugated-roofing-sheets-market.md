<p><ul><li>Bite Type</li><li>Lap Joint Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corrugated roofing sheets market types can be classified into three categories: bite type, lap joint type, and others. Bite type refers to sheets that have interlocking edges that bite into each other, creating a secure connection. Lap joint type sheets overlap each other, with one sheet covering the joint of the adjacent sheet. The "others" category includes any other type of corrugated roofing sheets that do not fall into the bite type or lap joint type. These market types determine the different installation methods and functionalities of corrugated roofing sheets.</p></p>
