<p><p>The Ultra thin FPC (Flexible Printed Circuit) market consists of two main types: single-sided and double-sided. In single-sided FPCs, the circuitry is present on one side of the flexible substrate, while in double-sided FPCs, the circuitry is present on both sides. Single-sided FPCs are commonly used when cost is a determining factor, while double-sided FPCs are preferred when there is a need for more complex circuitry and higher component density. Both types offer flexibility and compactness, making them ideal for applications where space is limited.</p></p>
