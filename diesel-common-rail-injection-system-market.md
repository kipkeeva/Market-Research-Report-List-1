<p><p>The Diesel Common Rail Injection System market includes two types of systems: solenoid type and piezo type. The solenoid type uses a solenoid coil to control the opening and closing of the injector nozzle, allowing the precise delivery of fuel to the engine. On the other hand, the piezo type uses a piezoelectric crystal to generate high-pressure pulses that control the fuel injection process with extreme precision. Both types contribute to increased fuel efficiency, reduced emissions, and improved engine performance.</p></p>
