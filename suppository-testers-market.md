<p><p>Suppository testers market offers various types of equipment for testing different properties of suppositories. Disintegration testers determine the time it takes for a suppository to break down in a simulated body environment. Hardness testers measure the resistance of a suppository to deformation. Melting point testers determine the temperature at which a suppository turns into a liquid. Penetration testers measure the ease of penetration of a suppository into body tissues. Lastly, dissolution test equipment evaluates the release rate of active ingredients from suppositories when exposed to a specified medium.</p></p>
