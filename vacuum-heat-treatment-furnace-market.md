<p><ul><li>Single Chamber Furnace</li><li>Double Chamber Furnace</li><li>Multi-chamber Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vacuum Heat Treatment Furnace market consists of three main types: Single Chamber Furnace, Double Chamber Furnace, and Multi-chamber Furnace. A Single Chamber Furnace is designed to accommodate one workload for heat treatment, providing uniform temperature and controlled atmosphere. A Double Chamber Furnace has two interconnected chambers, allowing simultaneous loading and unloading of materials, ensuring maximum utilization. Lastly, a Multi-chamber Furnace includes multiple chambers connected through a central vacuum pumping system, enabling parallel processing of different workloads, optimizing productivity and efficiency in heat treatment processes.</p></p>
