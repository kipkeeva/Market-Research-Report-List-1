<p><p>The market for curing agents for epoxy resin is categorized into three main types: amine-based epoxy hardeners, anhydride epoxy hardeners, and others. Amine-based epoxy hardeners are chemical compounds containing amine groups, which are added to epoxy resins to initiate the curing process. Anhydride epoxy hardeners, on the other hand, are compounds that react with epoxy resins in the presence of heat to accelerate the curing process. The category of "others" includes various curing agents that may be based on different chemical compositions or combinations, offering unique curing properties for epoxy resins.</p></p>
