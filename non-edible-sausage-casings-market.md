<p><p>The non-edible sausage casings market includes three main types: fibrous casings, collagen casings, and others. Fibrous casings are made from cellulose fibers and are ideal for cooked sausages as they provide good permeability, uniformity, and strength. Collagen casings are produced from animal collagen and offer excellent meat adhesion, elasticity, and uniform cooking. Other types of casings include plastic casings, cellulose casings, and natural casings like hog, sheep, or bovine intestines. These casings cater to specific market needs such as easier peeling, higher moisture retention, or specific product requirements.</p></p>
