<p><p>The dental laboratory furnace market consists of three main types of furnaces: sintering furnaces, burnout furnaces, and production furnaces. Sintering furnaces are used to heat ceramic materials at high temperatures to achieve proper bonding and strength. Burnout furnaces are designed to remove wax from molds before filling them with other materials. Production furnaces are used for a wide range of dental lab processes, including heating, curing, and hardening various materials like metals and ceramics. These furnaces play a crucial role in the dental industry by ensuring the fabrication of high-quality dental prosthetics and restorations.</p></p>
