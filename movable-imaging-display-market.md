<p><p>The Movable Imaging Display market consists of two popular types of displays: LED Display and OLED Display. LED Display utilizes an array of light-emitting diodes to create images, offering high brightness and excellent color reproduction. On the other hand, OLED Display uses organic compounds that emit light when an electric current is applied, resulting in flexible and thin displays with vibrant colors and deep blacks. Both types cater to different market needs, with LED Display commonly used for large-scale outdoor advertising and OLED Display preferred for portable devices and high-end televisions.</p></p>
