<p><p>The Automotive V2X Antenna market is divided into two primary types: Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) and Vehicle-to-Infrastructure (V2I). V2V involves communication between vehicles, enabling them to exchange important information like speed, position, and direction to enhance safety and avoid collisions. On the other hand, V2I facilitates communication between vehicles and roadside infrastructure such as traffic lights, road signs, and control systems. V2I enables vehicles to access real-time traffic updates, receive warnings, and optimize traffic flow, ultimately improving efficiency and reducing congestion on the road.</p></p>
