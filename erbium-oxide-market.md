<p><p>Erbium oxide, also known as erbium sesquioxide, is a pinkish-red powder that is a rare earth metal compound. The increasing demand for efficient telecommunication networks and the growing adoption of erbium-doped fiber amplifiers (EDFAs) are the primary drivers of this market.
