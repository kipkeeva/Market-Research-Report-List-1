<p><p>The integral waterproofing compound market consists of two main types: liquid-based and powder-based. Liquid-based compounds are typically mixed with water and applied as a coating on concrete surfaces, forming a protective barrier against water penetration. Powder-based compounds are added directly to the concrete mix during construction, enhancing its waterproofing properties. Both types offer effective waterproofing solutions for various applications, such as basements, roof decks, and swimming pools. These compounds are widely used in the construction industry to prevent water damage and increase the durability of concrete structures.</p></p>
