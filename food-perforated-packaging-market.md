PP offers high strength and resistance to moisture and heat, making it suitable for frozen food packaging. Perforated tarpaulin is commonly used for the packaging of bulk food items as it provides ventilation and protects against external factors. These packaging solutions ensure product quality, prolong shelf life, and meet safety standards in the food industry.</p></p>
