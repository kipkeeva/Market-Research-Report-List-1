<p><p>The Smart Sorting System market includes two main types: Linear Sorting Systems and Loop Sorting Systems. Linear Sorting Systems consist of a single line conveyor belt where items are placed and sorted based on predetermined criteria. These systems are best suited for small to medium-sized sorting operations. On the other hand, Loop Sorting Systems utilize a continuous loop conveyor belt, allowing for higher sorting capacity and faster processing. These systems are ideal for larger-scale sorting operations that require a high throughput rate.</p></p>
