<p><p>Dust monitoring systems can be categorized into two main types: portable and stationary. Portable dust monitoring systems are compact and lightweight devices that can be easily carried to different locations for on-site monitoring. They are ideal for short-term or temporary monitoring requirements. On the other hand, stationary dust monitoring systems are installed at specific locations for continuous monitoring over an extended period. They are typically used in industries or areas where dust emissions need to be continuously monitored for compliance with health and safety regulations.</p></p>
