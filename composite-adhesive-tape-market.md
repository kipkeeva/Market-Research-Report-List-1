<p><strong>The Composite Adhesive Tape Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Special Polyester-Based Adhesive Tape</li><li>Acetate-Based Adhesive Tape</li><li>Shrinkable Polyester Tape</li><li>Silicone-free Masking Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The composite adhesive tape market is categorized into four types. Special polyester-based adhesive tape is made using a polyester film coated with adhesive, providing high tensile strength and resistance to chemicals, heat, and moisture. Acetate-based adhesive tape is made using a tape with an acetate film coated with adhesive, offering good conformability and low electrical conductivity. Shrinkable polyester tape is made with heat-shrinkable polyester film that shrinks upon heating, providing a secure and tight fit. Silicone-free masking tape is a tape used for masking applications without leaving behind any silicone residue.</p></p>
