<p><ul><li>Injected Magnets</li><li>Pressed Magnets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ferrite bonded magnets are available in two main types: injected magnets and pressed magnets. Injected magnets are produced by injecting a mixture of ferrite powder and plastic binder into molds at high pressure. This process allows for the production of complex shapes and precise dimensions. On the other hand, pressed magnets are made by compacting ferrite powder and binder using a hydraulic press. This method is suitable for producing magnets with simpler shapes and lower precision requirements. Both types have their applications depending on the specific needs of the industry or product.</p></p>
