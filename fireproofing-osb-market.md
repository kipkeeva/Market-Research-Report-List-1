<p><p>The fireproofing OSB (oriented strand board) market comprises two main types: fireproofing additives and fireproofing coatings. Fireproofing additives are substances that can be mixed with OSB during the manufacturing process to enhance its fire-resistant properties. Fireproofing coatings, on the other hand, are applied on the surface of OSB after it is manufactured to provide an additional layer of fire protection. Both types offer solutions for improving the fire resistance of OSB materials and contribute to the overall safety in construction and other industries.</p></p>
