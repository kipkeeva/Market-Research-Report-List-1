<p><p>The pre-painted steel market can be categorized into three main types: Hot Dip Galvanised Steel (HDG), Galvanized Mild Steel (GMS), and Others. HDG refers to steel that has been coated with a layer of zinc through the process of hot-dip galvanization. GMS, on the other hand, involves coating mild steel with a layer of zinc through galvanization. The "Others" category encompasses any other types of pre-painted steel that do not fall under the HDG or GMS classifications. These different market types offer various characteristics and applications within the pre-painted steel industry.</p></p>
