<p><p>Galacto Oligosaccharides (GOS) are a type of prebiotic derived from lactose. GOS is commonly found in two market forms: syrup and powder. GOS syrup is a concentrated liquid form, obtained through a process of hydrolysis, where lactose is broken down into smaller oligosaccharide molecules. GOS powder, on the other hand, is a dried and powdered form obtained by evaporating the liquid syrup. Both syrup and powder market forms serve as functional ingredients in food and beverages, providing health benefits as prebiotics, stimulating the growth of beneficial gut bacteria.</p></p>
