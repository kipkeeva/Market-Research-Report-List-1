<p><ul><li>Rigid Forms</li><li>Colloidal Forms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hyaluronic acid bone filler market is categorized into two types: rigid forms and colloidal forms. Rigid forms refer to the solid or semi-solid fillers that are used to fill bone defects. They provide mechanical support and stability to the affected area. On the other hand, colloidal forms refer to the gel-like formulations that can flow easily into the defect site. They have a higher water content and can adapt better to irregular bone surfaces. Both types of fillers have their own advantages and are used based on the specific requirements of the patient.</p></p>
