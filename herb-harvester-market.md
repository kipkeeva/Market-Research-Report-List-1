<p><p>The herb harvester market consists of two main types: self-propelled herb harvesters and mounted herb harvesters. Self-propelled herb harvesters are machines that can move independently, allowing for efficient harvesting of herbs. They typically have wheels or tracks to navigate through fields. On the other hand, mounted herb harvesters are attached to a tractor or other agricultural vehicle. They are stationary and operated by being pulled through the fields by the vehicle. Both types of herb harvesters are designed to simplify and expedite the herb harvesting process.</p></p>
