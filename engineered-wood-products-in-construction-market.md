<p><p>Engineered wood products are becoming increasingly popular in the construction industry due to their versatility, strength, and cost-effectiveness. Plywood is a type of engineered wood made from thin layers of wood veneer bonded together, providing excellent strength and stability. Oriented Strand Board (OSB) is another option, made from wood strands compressed and glued together, known for its high strength and dimensional stability. Glued Laminated Timber (Glulam) involves bonding multiple layers of dimensioned lumber, offering exceptional strength and aesthetic appeal. Laminated Veneer Lumber (LVL) is made by stacking veneer sheets and bonding them under high heat and pressure, providing high strength and stiffness.</p></p>
