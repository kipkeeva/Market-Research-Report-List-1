<p><strong>The Portable X-Ray Inspection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FPD X-Ray Inspection System</li><li>Film X-Ray Inspection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable X-Ray Inspection System market is divided into two types: FPD X-Ray Inspection System and Film X-Ray Inspection System. FPD (Flat Panel Detector) X-Ray Inspection System utilizes a digital detector to capture X-ray images, replacing traditional films. It offers real-time imaging, high resolution, and enhanced image processing capabilities. On the other hand, Film X-Ray Inspection System employs traditional films to record X-ray images. Although it may lack some advanced features, it remains a cost-effective option in certain applications. Both systems serve the purpose of portable X-ray inspection, but differ in terms of image capturing technology.</p></p>
