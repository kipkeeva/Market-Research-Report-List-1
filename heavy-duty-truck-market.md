<p><p>The heavy-duty truck market includes three primary market types: complete vehicle, incomplete vehicle, and semitrailer tractor. An incomplete vehicle represents a partially assembled truck that requires additional manufacturing and customization to make it a functional vehicle. Finally, a semitrailer tractor is a heavy-duty truck specifically designed to pull a semitrailer, which is a trailer that lacks a front axle and relies on the tractor's front axle for support and steering.</p></p>
