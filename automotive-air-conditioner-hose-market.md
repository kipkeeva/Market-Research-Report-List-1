<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The automotive air conditioner hose market consists of various types, including the all-rubber type, barrier type, and veneer type. The all-rubber type hose is made entirely of rubber material, offering flexibility and durability. The barrier type hose has an inner layer made of rubber for flexibility and an outer layer made of nylon or polyester for enhanced resistance to refrigerant leakage. Lastly, the veneer type hose has a veneer layer made of polyester or nylon, providing additional protection against heat, oil, and ozone.</p></p>
