<p><p>The rubber compound market is categorized into various types such as EPDM compounding, SBR compounding, BR compounding, natural rubber compounding, NBR compounding, silicone rubber compounding, and others. EPDM compound refers to the compounding of rubber with ethylene propylene diene monomer, while SBR compound is made by combining styrene and butadiene. BR compound is produced by blending butadiene, while natural rubber compound is made from natural rubber latex. NBR compound involves the compounding of acrylonitrile and butadiene, while silicone rubber compound is created by mixing silicone polymers. The "others" category encompasses various other types of rubber compounding techniques.</p></p>
