<p><p>The automotive Intelligent Park Assist System (IPAS) market includes two types: semi-autonomous and fully autonomous systems. Semi-autonomous IPAS involves driver assistance while parking, where the system takes control of steering while the driver operates the gear and pedals. This system provides suggestions and guidance to park safely. On the other hand, fully autonomous IPAS enables the vehicle to park itself without any driver input. It utilizes sensors and cameras to detect obstacles, find a parking space, and maneuver smoothly into it. This advanced technology offers convenience and reduces the risk of accidents during parking.</p></p>
