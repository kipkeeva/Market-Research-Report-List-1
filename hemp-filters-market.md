<p><p>HEMP (Highly Effective Mega Power) filters are specifically designed to protect electrical systems from high-energy electromagnetic pulses (EMP). The market for HEMP filters is divided into two main types: Single Phase and Three Phase. </p><p>Single Phase HEMP filters are designed for use in single-phase electrical systems, which are typically found in residential and small commercial settings. These filters effectively mitigate the impact of EMP on single-phase electrical equipment by capturing and redirecting excessive energy.</p><p>On the other hand, Three Phase HEMP filters are designed for use in three-phase electrical systems, commonly employed in industrial and large commercial settings. These filters are capable of effectively handling the higher energy loads in three-phase systems and provide effective protection against EMP-induced damages, ensuring uninterrupted power supply and safeguarding equipment.</p></p>
