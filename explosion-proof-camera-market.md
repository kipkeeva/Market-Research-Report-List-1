<p><p>The Explosion Proof Camera market encompasses two types: Integrated Explosion Proof Camera and Split Type Explosion Proof Camera. Integrated Explosion Proof Cameras are designed with a fully enclosed structure, including the lens and pan-tilt, making them suitable for hazardous environments. On the other hand, Split Type Explosion Proof Cameras separate the lens and pan-tilt, connected by cables, enabling flexibility in installation. Both types are developed to operate in explosive areas while providing surveillance and security measures.</p></p>
