<p><ul><li>Direct Printing</li><li>Disperse Printing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct printing and disperse printing are two types of digital printing methods used in the fashion fabrics market. Direct printing involves using inkjet technology to directly print colors onto fabric, resulting in vibrant and detailed designs. Disperse printing, on the other hand, uses a heat transfer process to apply dye to synthetic fibers, allowing for a wide range of colors and intricate patterns. Both methods offer advantages such as faster production times, lower costs, and the ability to customize designs, making them popular choices in the fashion industry.</p></p>
