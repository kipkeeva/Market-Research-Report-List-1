Lipopeptides and lipoproteins, produced by microorganisms, have antimicrobial properties and find applications in various industries. Polymeric surfactants have long-chain, biodegradable molecules and are used in cleaning products and pharmaceuticals. Particulate biosurfactants are derived from microorganisms and are used in the oil and gas industry for enhanced oil recovery. These surfactants offer eco-friendly solutions while maintaining effective surface tension properties.</p></p>
