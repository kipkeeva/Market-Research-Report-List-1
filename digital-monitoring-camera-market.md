<p><ul><li>Infrared digital surveillance camera</li><li>Non-infrared digital surveillance camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital monitoring camera market can be categorized into two types: infrared digital surveillance cameras and non-infrared digital surveillance cameras. Infrared cameras utilize infrared light to capture images even in low-light or complete darkness, making them ideal for night surveillance. On the other hand, non-infrared cameras rely solely on regular visible light for image capture. Both camera types are widely used for monitoring purposes, with infrared cameras offering superior performance in low-light conditions, while non-infrared cameras are preferred for daylight surveillance.</p></p>
