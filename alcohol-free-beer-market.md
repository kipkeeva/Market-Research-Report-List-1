<p><strong>The Alcohol-free Beer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fermentation-limited Method</li><li>Dealcoholization Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The alcohol-free beer market comprises two main types of production methods: fermentation-limited method and dealcoholization method. In the fermentation-limited method, the brewing process intentionally restricts the alcohol content to a very low level. This is achieved by controlling the fermentation process to minimize the production of alcohol. On the other hand, the dealcoholization method involves producing regular beer with alcohol content and then removing the alcohol through various techniques such as vacuum distillation or reverse osmosis. Both methods enable the production of alcohol-free beer, providing options for consumers who prefer non-alcoholic beverages.</p></p>
