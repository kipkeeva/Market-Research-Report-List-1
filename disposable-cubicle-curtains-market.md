<p><p>The disposable cubicle curtains market can be categorized into two types - with mesh and without mesh. Curtains with mesh have a woven fabric with small holes, allowing for improved air circulation and visibility. These are commonly used in healthcare facilities, as they help reduce the spread of infections while still maintaining privacy. On the other hand, curtains without mesh are made of solid fabric and provide complete privacy. These are often used in settings where visibility and air circulation are not a priority, such as temporary workspaces or storage areas.</p></p>
