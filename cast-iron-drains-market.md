<p><p>The Cast Iron Drains market can be categorized into two types: Flexible Interface Drain and Rigid Interface Drain. The Flexible Interface Drain is designed to provide flexibility in installation, allowing for slight adjustments and movement. It is suitable for areas with potential ground movement or settling. On the other hand, the Rigid Interface Drain offers a fixed installation, usually used in areas where stability is crucial. Both types serve the purpose of efficiently draining water and maintaining effective water management systems.</p></p>
