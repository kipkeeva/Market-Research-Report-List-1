<p><p>4K LCD displays refer to liquid crystal display technology that has a resolution of 3840 x 2160 pixels. HDR enables displays to deliver a broader range of colors and higher contrast ratios, enhancing the viewing experience. OLED technology, on the other hand, offers better color reproduction, wider viewing angles, and faster response times.</p><p>The market is also witnessing the increasing adoption of 4K displays in numerous applications, including televisions, monitors, digital signage, laptops, and smartphones.
