<p><p>Car drum brakes are a type of braking system that consists of a drum-shaped housing, brake shoes, and a hydraulic braking mechanism. When the driver presses the brake pedal, hydraulic pressure forces the brake shoes against the inner surface of the drum, creating friction and slowing down the rotation of the wheel.</p><p>The global car drum brakes market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.4% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to several factors, including the increasing demand for automobiles in emerging economies, the rising emphasis on vehicle safety, and the lower cost of drum brake systems compared to disc brakes.</p><p>One of the key trends in the car drum brakes market is the growing adoption of advanced materials and technologies. For instance, some manufacturers are incorporating lightweight materials in the construction of drum brakes to improve fuel efficiency and reduce the overall weight of the vehicle. Additionally, advancements in hydraulic braking systems, such as the integration of electronic brake force distribution (EBD) and anti-lock braking system (ABS), are also driving the demand for car drum brakes.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing demand for electric vehicles (EVs) is expected to boost the car drum brakes market. EVs often require a combination of drum and disc brakes, as drum brakes are more suitable for the rear wheels due to their ability to handle high torque loads and provide better parking brake functionality.</p><p>In conclusion, the car drum brakes market is projected to experience significant growth in the coming years. The adoption of advanced materials and technologies, combined with the increasing demand for EVs and emphasis on vehicle safety, are key factors driving this growth.</p></p>
