It offers excellent thermal and mechanical properties, including high strength and stiffness, as well as chemical resistance. Pure PEEK resin is widely used in applications that require strong and durable materials, such as automotive, aerospace, and oil and gas industries.</p><p>Modified Resin: Modified PEEK resin is a variant of pure PEEK that has been enhanced with additives to further improve its performance. These modifications can include reinforcement fibres, fillers, or coatings that provide specific properties like increased wear resistance, enhanced electrical conductivity, or improved heat resistance. Modified PEEK resins are tailored for specific applications, such as medical implants, semiconductor manufacturing, and high-performance bearings, where superior properties are crucial.</p></p>
