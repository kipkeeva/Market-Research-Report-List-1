<p><p>The solvent market can be categorized into two types: organic solvents and inorganic solvents. Organic solvents are derived from carbon-based compounds and are widely used in various industries. They have high dissolving properties and are commonly used for cleaning, degreasing, and dissolving materials. Inorganic solvents, on the other hand, are not derived from carbon and are commonly used in industries such as chemicals, metallurgy, and electronics. They are known for their high boiling points and excellent heat transfer properties. Both types of solvents play crucial roles in several industrial applications.</p></p>
