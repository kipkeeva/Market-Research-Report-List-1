<p><p>PFA Lined Centrifugal Pumps are a type of industrial pumps used for various applications, particularly in corrosive environments. There are two types of PFA lined centrifugal pumps, namely Magnetic Drive Pump and Mechanical Seal Pump. Magnetic Drive Pump utilizes magnets to drive the impeller, eliminating the need for a traditional shaft seal, thus preventing leakage. On the other hand, Mechanical Seal Pump uses a rotating shaft seal to prevent any leakage. Both types are commonly used in industries where the transfer of corrosive or hazardous fluids is required.</p></p>
