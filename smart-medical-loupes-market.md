TTL Loupes are permanently fixed to the frame and provide a seamless viewing experience. On the other hand, Flip-up Loupes can be flipped away from the eyes when not in use, which allows for better flexibility and convenience. Both types of loupes offer improved precision and accuracy, helping medical professionals perform their duties more effectively.</p></p>
