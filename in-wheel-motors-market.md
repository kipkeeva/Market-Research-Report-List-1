<p><strong>The In-Wheel Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner Rotor Type</li><li>Outer Rotor Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The In-Wheel Motors market can be classified into two main types: Inner Rotor Type and Outer Rotor Type. The Inner Rotor Type refers to motors where the rotating part is positioned inside the stator. This design allows for a compact size and high torque output, making it suitable for electric vehicle applications. On the other hand, the Outer Rotor Type has the rotating part positioned outside the stator. This type offers better heat dissipation and can provide higher power outputs, making it ideal for heavy-duty electric vehicles or hybrid vehicles.</p></p>
