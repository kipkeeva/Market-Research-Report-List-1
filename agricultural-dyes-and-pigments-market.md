<p><p>The agricultural dyes and pigments market consists of two main types: dyes and pigments. Dyes are soluble substances that provide color to agricultural products. They are often used in applications such as seed coatings, fertilizers, and plant protection products. On the other hand, pigments are insoluble and provide color by reflecting light. They are mainly used in applications like mulches, wraps, and films to protect crops from sunlight. Both dyes and pigments play a crucial role in enhancing visual appeal, identification, and differentiation of agricultural products in the market.</p></p>
