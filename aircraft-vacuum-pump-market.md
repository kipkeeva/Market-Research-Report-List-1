<p><p>The Aircraft Vacuum Pump market consists of two main types: OEMs (Original Equipment Manufacturers) and Aftermarket. OEMs refer to the companies that produce vacuum pumps as a part of the aircraft's original manufacturing process. These pumps are typically installed by the aircraft manufacturer itself. On the other hand, Aftermarket refers to the market for vacuum pumps that are sold separately after the initial sale of the aircraft. These pumps are usually purchased by aircraft operators to replace or upgrade existing pumps.</p></p>
