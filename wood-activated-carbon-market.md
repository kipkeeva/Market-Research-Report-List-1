<p><p>Wood activated carbon is a type of adsorbent material made from wood. It is available in two main market types: powdered and granular. Powdered wood activated carbon refers to fine particles of the material that have a larger surface area, making it highly effective in adsorbing gases and liquids. It is commonly used in liquid phase applications such as water purification and wastewater treatment. On the other hand, granular wood activated carbon consists of larger particles and is more commonly used in gas phase applications in industries such as air purification and gas masks.</p></p>
