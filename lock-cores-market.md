<p><ul><li>Uncombinated Cores</li><li>Combinated Cores</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lock cores market is divided into two main types: uncombinated cores and combinated cores. Uncombinated cores are locks that are sold without key combinations, allowing the locksmith or end user to create customized keying systems. These cores are commonly used in commercial settings where different keys are needed for different areas. On the other hand, combinated cores are locks that come pre-assembled with key combinations. These are often utilized in residential settings where a single key is required to operate multiple locks.</p></p>
