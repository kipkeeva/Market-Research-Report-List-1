<p><p>UAV ESCs (Electronic Speed Controllers) are an essential component of drones, controlling the motors' speed and direction. There are two common types of UAV ESCs on the market – BEC ESCs and OPTO ESCs. BEC ESCs (Battery Eliminator Circuit) are equipped with a built-in BEC feature, which provides power to the drone's flight controller and other electronics. On the other hand, OPTO ESCs do not have a BEC and require an external power source for the flight controller. The choice between BEC and OPTO ESCs depends on the specific requirements of the drone's setup and electronics.</p></p>
