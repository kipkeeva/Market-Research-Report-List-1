<p><p>Dental suspenders, also known as dental support arches, are dental appliances used to prevent orthodontic relapse and stabilize teeth positioning after orthodontic treatment. <p><p>Dental suspenders are used for holding the tongue or cheek away from the surgical area during dental procedures. There are three main types of dental suspenders based on the materials used: metal, ceramics, and polymer. Metal suspenders are typically made of stainless steel or titanium and provide strong and durable support. Ceramic suspenders are made from ceramic materials and offer good aesthetic appeal while maintaining strength. Polymer suspenders are made from synthetic materials and are known for their flexibility and comfort. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the dental procedure.</p></p>
