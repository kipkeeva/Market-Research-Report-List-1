<p><p>A children sphygmomanometer is a medical device used to measure blood pressure in children. It consists of an inflatable cuff that is wrapped around the arm and connected to a pressure gauge. This allows healthcare professionals to assess a child's blood pressure and identify any potential health issues.</p><p>The market for children sphygmomanometers is expected to grow at a significant rate in the coming years.
