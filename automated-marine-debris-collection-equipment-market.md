<p><p>Automated marine debris collection equipment can be categorized into three types - collect, smash, and uninstall. The collect equipment is designed to efficiently gather marine debris from the ocean surface or from the seabed. Smash equipment, on the other hand, is specifically built to break down larger debris items into smaller pieces, making it easier for collection or disposal. Lastly, uninstall equipment is intended to remove underwater structures, such as oil rigs or submarine debris, from the marine environment. These different types of equipment contribute to the overall efforts in combating marine pollution and maintaining the health of our oceans.</p></p>
