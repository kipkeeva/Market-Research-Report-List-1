<p><p>Pour point depressants are additives used in crude oil to improve its low-temperature flow properties. In the polymer type market, polymers like ethylene-vinyl acetate copolymers are used as additives, which function by altering wax crystal formation and reducing their ability to form a network, preventing the oil from gelling. Surfactant type market involves the use of surface-active agents that interact with wax crystals and modify their shape, preventing the formation of a gel structure. Compound type market incorporates additives like alkylbenzenes and alkyl naphthalenes, which interact with wax crystals and hinder their agglomeration, ensuring smooth oil flow at low temperatures.</p></p>
