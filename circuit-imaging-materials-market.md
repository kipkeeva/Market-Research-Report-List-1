<p><ul><li>Dry Film Photoresist</li><li>Screen Printing Ink</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The circuit imaging materials market primarily consists of three types of materials: dry film photoresist, screen printing ink, and others. Dry film photoresist is a light-sensitive material used to transfer circuit patterns onto substrates. Screen printing ink is a paste-like material used to create conductive patterns on circuit boards. The "others" category includes various materials like solder masks and etchants used in the circuit imaging process. These materials play a crucial role in the production of electronic circuits and contribute to the efficiency and reliability of electronic devices.</p></p>
