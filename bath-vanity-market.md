<p><p>The bath vanity market comprises two main types: single sink and double sink vanities. Single sink vanities are designed to accommodate and support a single sink basin, offering ample countertop space and storage options. They are ideal for smaller bathrooms or spaces where only one person regularly uses the vanity area. In contrast, double sink vanities are designed to accommodate two sink basins, providing separate areas for two individuals to use simultaneously. They are typically found in larger bathrooms or shared spaces where multiple users require their own sink and storage space.</p></p>
