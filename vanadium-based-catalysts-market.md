<p><p>Vanadium-based catalysts are widely used in various industries due to their exceptional catalytic properties. Two types of vanadium-based catalysts are γ-alumina vanadium-based catalysts and pseudo-boehmite vanadium-based catalysts. γ-alumina vanadium-based catalysts are composed of gamma-alumina and vanadium oxide, providing high surface area and thermal stability. Pseudo-boehmite vanadium-based catalysts, on the other hand, consist of pseudo-boehmite and vanadium oxide, exhibiting excellent catalytic activity and selectivity. Both catalyst types play a crucial role in promoting chemical reactions and are widely utilized in petroleum refining, chemical processing, and environmental applications.</p></p>
