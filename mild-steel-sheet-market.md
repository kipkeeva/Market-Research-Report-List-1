<p><p>The Mild Steel Sheet market encompasses two main types: Hot Roll Mild Steel Sheet and Cold Roll Mild Steel Sheet. Hot Roll Mild Steel Sheet is produced through a rolling process at high temperatures, resulting in a smooth surface and increased ductility. This type is commonly used in construction, automotive, and manufacturing industries. On the other hand, Cold Roll Mild Steel Sheet is manufactured at lower temperatures, which enhances its strength and hardness. It is often utilized in applications that require precise dimensions and a smooth finish, such as appliances and electrical enclosures.</p></p>
