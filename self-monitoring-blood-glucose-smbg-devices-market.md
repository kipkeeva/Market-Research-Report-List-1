<p><p>Self-monitoring blood glucose (SMBG) devices are essential tools for individuals with diabetes to manage their condition. These devices include three key components: test strips, glucose meters, and lancets. Test strips are small strips made of plastic that contain chemicals to measure glucose levels in blood. Glucose meters are handheld devices used to read the glucose levels from the test strips. Lancets are small needles used to prick the skin, allowing for blood sample collection. Together, these components enable individuals to monitor their blood sugar levels regularly and make informed decisions about their diabetes management.</p></p>
