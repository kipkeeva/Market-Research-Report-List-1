<p><p>The market for automatic circular sawing machines can be divided into two types: semi-automatic and fully automatic machines. Semi-automatic circular sawing machines require some manual input, such as loading and unloading materials, but the cutting process is automated. On the other hand, fully automatic circular sawing machines are capable of performing all cutting tasks without any manual intervention. Both types of machines cater to different customer needs based on their level of automation and are widely used in various industries, including metalworking and woodworking.</p></p>
