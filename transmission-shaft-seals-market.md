<p><p>Transmission shaft seals are essential components in machinery that prevent the leakage of fluids and contaminants from the transmission system. Radial shaft seals are circular seals that are used to seal rotating shafts and prevent lubricating oil from leaking out. Metal face seals are specialized seals with metal-to-metal contact, ensuring high durability and resistance to harsh conditions. V-ring seals are flexible rubber seals that are installed on shafts to retain lubricants and exclude contaminants. Other types of transmission shaft seals include rotary shaft seals, felt seals, and labyrinth seals, each suitable for specific applications.</p></p>
