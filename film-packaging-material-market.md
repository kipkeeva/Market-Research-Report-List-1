<p><ul><li>Rigid Film Packaging Material</li><li>Flexible Film Packaging Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Film packaging material is divided into two main types: rigid film packaging material and flexible film packaging material. </p><p>Rigid film packaging material refers to materials that are firm and inflexible, such as plastic bottles or trays. These materials provide strength and stability to protect products from external damages and preserve their quality.</p><p>On the other hand, flexible film packaging material includes materials that are pliable and can bend, like plastic wraps or pouches. These materials offer versatility and convenience, allowing for easy sealing and access to the packaged product.</p></p>
