<p><p>Vehicle passive safety systems are designed to protect vehicle occupants in the event of an accident. Unlike active safety systems that aim to prevent accidents, passive safety systems focus on minimizing the risk of injury in case of a collision. These sensors enable the system to detect potential accidents and activate safety features such as airbags and seat belt pretensioners to protect the occupants.
