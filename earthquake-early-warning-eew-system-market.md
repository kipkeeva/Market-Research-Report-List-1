<p><p>The Earthquake Early Warning (EEW) System market consists of three main types: Earthquake Sensor, Servers, and Receivers. Earthquake Sensors are devices that detect seismic activities and gather data related to earthquakes. Servers are responsible for processing and analyzing the data collected by the sensors. They play a crucial role in generating accurate earthquake warnings. Receivers are used to disseminate the warnings to various stakeholders, such as government agencies, emergency services, and the public. Together, these components form a comprehensive EEW system that ensures timely alerts and mitigates the impact of earthquakes.</p></p>
