<p><ul><li>Sensor</li><li>Operating Units</li><li>Control Module (ECU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Transmission Electronics Market includes three main types: sensors, operating units, and control modules (ECU). Sensors play a crucial role by collecting data from various parts of the transmission system, such as speed, temperature, and pressure. Operating units, like shift levers and switches, allow drivers to engage different gears and control the transmission. Control modules (ECU) process the information received from sensors and operating units to make necessary adjustments and ensure smooth functioning of the transmission system. These components are essential for the proper functioning and efficiency of automotive transmissions.</p></p>
