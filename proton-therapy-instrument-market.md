<p><p>The Proton Therapy Instrument market includes several types of equipment used in delivering proton therapy treatment. These include synchrotrons, cyclotrons, synchrocyclotrons, and linear accelerators. Synchrotrons are large machines that accelerate protons using magnetic fields and high voltages. Cyclotrons are also particle accelerators but use a different method to generate protons. Synchrocyclotrons combine elements of both synchrotrons and cyclotrons. Lastly, linear accelerators (linacs) use electric fields to accelerate protons in a straight line. Each of these instruments has its own advantages and limitations, leading to different applications and market demand.</p></p>
