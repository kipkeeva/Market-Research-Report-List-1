<p><p>Integral color admixtures are additives used in the concrete industry to create consistent and long-lasting color throughout the concrete. They are available in two main forms: powder and liquid. Powder integral color admixtures consist of pigment particles mixed with a dry carrier material. These powders are typically added to the concrete mix during batching. On the other hand, liquid integral color admixtures are pre-dispersed pigments in a liquid carrier, which are easily mixed into the concrete during the mixing process. Both types offer a wide range of color options and are used to enhance the aesthetic appeal of concrete surfaces.</p></p>
