<p><p>Melamine-faced panels for flooring, also known as MF-C and MF-MDF, are two market types within the industry. MF-C refers to melamine-faced chipboard panels, made by compressing wood chips together with a melamine resin overlay. These panels are known for their durability and affordability, making them a popular choice for flooring installations. On the other hand, MF-MDF stands for melamine-faced medium-density fiberboard panels. These panels are made using wood fibers mixed with a resin and formed under high pressure and temperature, resulting in a smooth and consistent surface suitable for flooring applications.</p></p>
