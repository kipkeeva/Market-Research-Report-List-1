<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Magnesium Oxide market can be categorized into different types. Dead-Burned Magnesium Oxide (DBM) is produced by heating magnesium carbonate under high temperatures, resulting in a dense, highly stable form. Fused Magnesium Oxide (FM) is obtained by melting natural magnesite, eventually forming a smooth and porous texture. Caustic Calcined Magnesium Oxide (CCM) is created by treating magnesium oxide with a solution of sodium hydroxide. Lastly, Synthetic Magnesium Oxide is manufactured through a chemical process using magnesium chloride or magnesium sulfate as raw materials.</p></p>
