<p><p>The general-purpose diamond saw blades market offers different types of blades for various cutting applications. High-frequency welding involves joining the diamond segments to the blade through the use of high-frequency energy, ensuring strong adhesion. Laser welding utilizes laser beams to fuse the diamond segments, resulting in a precise and durable blade. All three types provide different advantages and cater to specific cutting requirements in the market.</p></p>
