<p><p>The galvanized round pipe market includes different types such as ordinary steel and thickened steel. Ordinary steel pipes are made using standard carbon steel and are commonly used for general purposes like plumbing and construction. On the other hand, thickened steel pipes are manufactured with a higher gauge thickness, providing them with increased durability and strength. These pipes are used in applications that require higher pressure handling, such as gas transmission and industrial infrastructure projects. Both types of pipes are coated with a layer of zinc to enhance their corrosion resistance.</p></p>
