<p><p>The Clear Borosilicate Glass Tube market can be classified into two types: Low Borosilicate and Medium Borosilicate. Low Borosilicate glass tubes have a lower coefficient of expansion and are commonly used in applications that require resistance to thermal shock, such as laboratory glassware and lighting fixtures. On the other hand, Medium Borosilicate glass tubes have a slightly higher coefficient of expansion and are typically used in applications that require higher temperature resistance, such as certain types of cookware and industrial equipment. Overall, these classifications cater to specific industry needs for different levels of thermal resistance.</p></p>
