Phase change thermal insulation coatings utilize materials that change phase between solid and liquid to absorb and release heat energy efficiently. Vacuum thermal insulation coatings create a vacuum layer to minimize heat transfer. Reflective thermal insulation coatings reflect sunlight and heat, preventing them from penetrating the surface. Other types of energy efficient coatings include those with specific chemical formulations aimed at reducing heat transfer.</p></p>
