<p><p>The aviation titanium alloy market includes two primary types: aircraft structure titanium alloy and engine titanium alloy. It is highly sought after due to its lightweight yet strong properties, helping to reduce the overall weight of the aircraft. On the other hand, engine titanium alloy is utilized in the production of engine components like blades, discs, and casings. Its high temperature resistance and strength make it a preferred choice for aerospace engines.</p></p>
