<p><ul><li>Active Feed</li><li>Manual Feed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Lock Screw Machine market can be categorized into two types: Active Feed and Manual Feed. In the Active Feed market, the machine automatically feeds the screws into the locking mechanism. It eliminates the need for manual handling of screws, resulting in increased efficiency and productivity. On the other hand, the Manual Feed market requires operators to manually feed the screws into the machine. This type may be preferable for applications that involve specialized or delicate screws that cannot be easily handled by automated systems.</p></p>
