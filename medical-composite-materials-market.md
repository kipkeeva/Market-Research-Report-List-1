<p><p>Medical composite materials are used in various medical applications due to their unique properties and capabilities. Fiber composites consist of high-strength fibers reinforced with a polymer matrix, providing enhanced strength and durability. Polymer-metal composites combine the properties of both polymers and metals, offering excellent biocompatibility and mechanical strength. Polymer-ceramic composites combine polymers with ceramic fillers, providing superior heat resistance and biocompatibility. These different types of composite materials are extensively used in medical devices, implants, prosthetics, and drug delivery systems to improve patient outcomes and enhance medical procedures.</p></p>
