<p><p>The capacitive touch stylus pen market can be broadly classified into two types: active capacitive touch stylus pens and passive capacitive touch stylus pens. </p><p>Active capacitive touch stylus pens have an electronic component that requires a power source, such as a battery, to function. These stylus pens are more sensitive and provide a more precise and accurate writing experience on touchscreens.</p><p>Passive capacitive touch stylus pens, on the other hand, do not require a power source as they use a conductive material, generally rubber or metal, to interact with the touchscreen. While they may be less sensitive than active stylus pens, they are generally more affordable and widely available in the market.</p></p>
