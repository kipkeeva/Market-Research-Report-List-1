<p><strong>The Offset Print Label Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Sensitive</li><li>Glue-applied</li><li>Heat-Shrink and Stretch Sleeve</li><li>In-mold Labels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The offset print label market is divided into four main types: pressure sensitive, glue-applied, heat-shrink and stretch sleeve, and in-mold labels. Pressure sensitive labels are adhesive-backed and can be easily applied to various surfaces. Glue-applied labels require a specific type of glue for application. Heat-shrink and stretch sleeve labels are printed on a film that shrinks or stretches tightly around a container when heated. In-mold labels are embedded into the packaging during the manufacturing process, resulting in a seamless appearance. These different types cater to varying labeling needs in the market.</p></p>
