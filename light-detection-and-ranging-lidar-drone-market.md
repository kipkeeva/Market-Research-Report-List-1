<p><p>The light detection and ranging (LiDAR) drone market consists of two primary types: rotary-wing LiDAR drones and fixed-wing LiDAR drones. Rotary-wing LiDAR drones are equipped with multi-rotor systems and offer vertical takeoff and landing capabilities, making them suitable for operations in confined spaces and complex terrains. Conversely, fixed-wing LiDAR drones have a traditional aircraft design with fixed wings, enabling longer flight durations and larger coverage areas, making them ideal for large scale mapping and surveying projects. Both types of drones utilize LiDAR technology for remote sensing and data collection purposes.</p></p>
