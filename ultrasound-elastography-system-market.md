<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ultrasound Elastography System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Elastography (SE)</li><li>Dynamic Elastography With Harmonic Stimuli (DEHS)</li><li>Dynamic Elastography With Transient Stimuli (DETS)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasound elastography systems are categorized into different types based on the methods used for measuring tissue elasticity. </p><p>Static elastography (SE) involves applying external pressure on the tissue and measuring the resulting deformation to determine its elasticity. </p><p>Dynamic elastography with harmonic stimuli (DEHS) utilizes high-frequency waves to produce vibrations in the tissue, and the resulting displacement is measured to assess elasticity. </p><p>Dynamic elastography with transient stimuli (DETS) uses mechanical waves or acoustic radiation force to generate tissue displacement, providing information about tissue stiffness. </p><p>Other types of elastography systems may include different methodologies or variations of the aforementioned techniques.</p></p>
