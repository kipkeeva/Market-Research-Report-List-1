<p><strong>The Water Disinfection Ultraviolet Lamp Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Pressure</li><li>Medium Pressure</li><li>High Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water disinfection ultraviolet lamp market is categorized into different types based on the pressure of the lamp. Low-pressure UV lamps use low electrical current and produce UV-C radiation primarily at a wavelength of 254 nanometers. Medium-pressure UV lamps emit UV-C, UV-B, and UV-A radiation at various wavelengths through the use of a mercury vapor that is ignited by an electric discharge. High-pressure UV lamps generate a broader spectrum of UV radiation, including UV-C, UV-B, and UV-A, using a more intense electrical discharge in a quartz or fused silica envelope.</p></p>
