<p><p>The High Purity Sulfuric Acid market is segmented into four types: G2, G3, G4, and G5. G2 sulfuric acid offers a sulfur trioxide (SO3) content of over 99% and is primarily used in the manufacture of dyes, pigments, and specialty chemicals. G3 sulfuric acid has a SO3 content of 98-99% and finds applications in the pharmaceutical industry. G4 sulfuric acid contains 95-98% SO3 and is utilized in oil refining and wastewater treatment. G5 sulfuric acid, with a SO3 content below 95%, is used for cleaning and washing purposes.</p></p>
