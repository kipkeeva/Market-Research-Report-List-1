<p><p>Glass roofing is available in various types, including ceramic frit glass, silicone coated glass, and others. Ceramic frit glass is created by applying a layer of ceramic paint onto the glass surface before it is heat-treated, providing control over sunlight penetration and glare reduction. Silicone coated glass involves a layer of silicone coating on the glass, enhancing its durability and resistance to weathering. Other types may include laminated glass, tinted glass, or low-e glass, offering additional features like insulation, UV protection, or aesthetics for different roofing applications.</p></p>
