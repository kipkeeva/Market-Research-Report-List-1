<p><p>Styrene-Maleic Anhydride (SMA) copolymer market can be categorized into two types: A-SMA and R-SMA. A-SMA refers to acid-modified SMA copolymers, which possess high solubility in water and are used as dispersants, emulsion stabilizers, and wetting agents. On the other hand, R-SMA stands for resin-modified SMA copolymers that have low water solubility and find applications in various industries including adhesives, coatings, and composites. Both A-SMA and R-SMA offer unique properties and performance benefits and are extensively utilized in specific sectors within the chemical industry.</p></p>
