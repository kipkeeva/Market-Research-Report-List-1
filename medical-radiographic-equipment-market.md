<p><strong>The Medical Radiographic Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CR Technology</li><li>DR Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical radiographic equipment market is segmented into two types: Computed Radiography (CR) Technology and Digital Radiography (DR) Technology. CR technology involves the use of imaging plates to capture X-ray images, which are then scanned and converted into digital form. DR technology, on the other hand, directly captures digital X-ray images using detectors. Both technologies have their advantages and limitations. CR technology is cost-effective and allows for image enhancement, while DR technology provides immediate image results with improved image quality, faster workflow, and reduced radiation exposure.</p></p>
