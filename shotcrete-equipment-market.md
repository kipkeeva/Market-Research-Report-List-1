<p><p>The Shotcrete Equipment Market can be categorized into two main types based on the technology used, namely wet mix and dry mix. Wet mix shotcrete involves mixing all the ingredients together before being sprayed onto the desired surface, while dry mix shotcrete involves adding water to the dry ingredients at the nozzle during spraying. Furthermore, the market can also be classified based on the product type, which includes manual spraying equipment and robotic spraying equipment. Manual spraying equipment is operated by humans, whereas robotic spraying equipment is automated and controlled remotely.</p></p>
