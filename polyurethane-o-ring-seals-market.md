<p><p>Polyurethane O-Ring Seals are widely used in various industries for effective sealing applications. Non-reinforced seals are made solely from polyurethane, providing excellent resistance to abrasion, chemicals, and wear. These seals are suitable for low-pressure applications. On the other hand, fabric-reinforced seals are composed of polyurethane with a fabric layer embedded within. This reinforcement enhances the seal's strength and stability, making it ideal for high-pressure and dynamic applications. Both types of seals offer reliable sealing solutions for different requirements within the market.</p></p>
