<p><p>A benchtop spectrum analyzer is a high-precision instrument used for analyzing and measuring the frequency spectrum of electronic signals. With the proliferation of wireless devices and the need for efficient spectrum allocation, telecom providers are investing in advanced spectrum analyzers to ensure optimum utilization of frequency bands.
