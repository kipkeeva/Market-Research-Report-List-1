<p><p>Heat resistance glass is a type of glass that can withstand high temperatures without breaking or shattering. There are three main types of heat resistant glass: tempered glass, Pyrex glass, and Pyroceram glass. Tempered glass is made by heating and cooling it quickly, making it four to five times stronger than regular glass. Pyrex glass is made by adding boron to the glass composition, making it highly resistant to thermal shock. Pyroceram glass is a ceramic material with exceptional thermal resistance, capable of withstanding extreme temperatures.</p></p>
