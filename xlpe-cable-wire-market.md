<p><p>XLPE (cross-linked polyethylene) cable and wire market can be categorized into different types based on their voltage rating. MV (medium voltage) cables are designed for larger power distribution networks, such as urban areas and industrial zones. HV (high voltage) cables are used for transmitting electricity over longer distances, while EHV (extra high voltage) cables are utilized in ultra-high voltage transmission systems. Each type serves specific purposes within the overall power distribution infrastructure.</p></p>
