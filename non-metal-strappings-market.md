<p><p>Non-metal strapping is widely used in various industries for packaging and securing goods. Two common types of non-metal strappings are PP strapping and PET strapping. PP strapping, made of polypropylene, is lightweight, cost-effective, and suitable for light to medium-duty applications. PET strapping, made of polyester, is stronger, more rigid, and capable of handling heavy-duty applications. Both types offer excellent tensile strength, durability, and resistance to weather and UV radiation. They are preferred choices for industries such as logistics, shipping, manufacturing, and construction for efficient and secure packaging solutions.</p></p>
