<p><p>The fire retardant cladding market encompasses four main types: metal, composite, concrete, and wooden cladding. Metal cladding, often made of aluminum or steel, offers durability and resistance to high temperatures. Composite cladding combines different materials, such as wood and plastic, to provide both aesthetic appeal and fire resistance. Concrete cladding is known for its strength and durability, making it a popular choice in high-rise buildings. Wooden cladding, on the other hand, provides a natural and timeless look but requires additional fire retardant treatments to enhance its resistance to flames.</p></p>
