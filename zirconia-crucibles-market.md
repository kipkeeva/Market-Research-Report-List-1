<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the Zirconia Crucibles market, Type I refers to crucibles made from partially stabilized zirconia (PSZ), which offer high thermal shock resistance and excellent chemical resistance. They are widely used in applications such as thermal analysis and metal casting. On the other hand, Type II crucibles are made from fully stabilized zirconia and possess exceptional mechanical strength and stability, making them suitable for demanding applications like molten metal handling and extreme temperature environments. Both types cater to different industrial needs and offer specific advantages based on their unique material composition.</p></p>
