<p><p>Pressure Independent Balancing Control Valves (PIBCVs) are devices used in HVAC systems to maintain a constant flow rate regardless of variations in pressure. In the market, there are two common types of PIBCVs: Thread and Flange. </p><p>Threaded PIBCVs have screw-like threads on their connectors, allowing them to be easily screwed onto pipes or fittings. This type is commonly used in smaller diameter pipes or where retrofitting is required.</p><p>Flanged PIBCVs, on the other hand, have flat flanges on their connectors, enabling them to be bolted onto pipes or fittings. This type is commonly used in larger diameter pipes or when higher pressure applications are needed.</p></p>
