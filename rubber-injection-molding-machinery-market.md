<p><p>The Rubber Injection Molding Machinery market is categorized into two types: Vertical Type and Horizontal Type. On the other hand, Horizontal Type machinery is designed with a horizontal mold, where the mold opens and closes horizontally. This type of machinery is commonly used for producing larger rubber products and is preferred for its higher production efficiency.</p></p>
