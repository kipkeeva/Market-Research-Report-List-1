<p><p>The reusable diapers market offers various types of diapers to suit different preferences. Pocket type diapers have a waterproof outer layer with a pocket inside to insert an absorbent insert. Fitted type diapers are made of multiple layers of absorbent materials and need a separate waterproof cover. All-in-one type diapers consist of a single unit with the absorbent layer sewn into a waterproof cover. All-in-two type diapers have an absorbent insert that snaps into a waterproof shell. Other types include swim diapers, pre-folds, and hybrid diapers that can be used with disposable inserts.</p></p>
