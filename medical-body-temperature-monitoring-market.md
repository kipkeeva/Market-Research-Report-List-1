<p><p>The medical body temperature monitoring market consists of two main types: wired and wireless. On the other hand, the wireless market utilizes wireless technologies such as Bluetooth or Wi-Fi to transmit temperature data to a centralized device. Wireless monitoring offers greater convenience and flexibility as patients can move freely, while wired monitoring ensures continuous and reliable data transmission. Both types play a crucial role in monitoring patients' body temperature in medical settings.</p></p>
