<p><p>Tabletop paper folding machines are popular office equipment used to fold papers efficiently. There are two main types available: 2-plate paper folding machines and 4-plate paper folding machines. The 2-plate machines have two folding plates that fold the paper in half, creating a simple center fold. On the other hand, 4-plate machines offer more folding options as they have four plates, allowing for various types of folds such as letter, accordion, or double parallel folds. Both types bring convenience and precision to paper folding tasks in offices and other professional settings.</p></p>
