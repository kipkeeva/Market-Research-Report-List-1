<p><ul><li>Polymer Laminate</li><li>All-solid-state</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrochromic window market comprises two main types: polymer laminate and all-solid-state. Polymer laminate electrochromic windows consist of multiple layers of polymer materials that can change their color and opacity when an electric current is applied. On the other hand, all-solid-state electrochromic windows use inorganic materials and do not require a liquid or gel electrolyte. Both types offer benefits such as energy efficiency, glare reduction, and privacy control. However, they differ in their manufacturing processes and technology, resulting in variations in cost and performance.</p></p>
