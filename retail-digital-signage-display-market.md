<p><p>The retail digital signage display market consists of two main types: LED panel and LCD panel. LED panels use light-emitting diodes to produce images and are known for their vibrant colors, high brightness, and energy efficiency. They offer superior contrast and are ideal for outdoor and large-scale displays. On the other hand, LCD panels use liquid crystal technology to display images, offering high resolution and wide viewing angles. They are commonly used in indoor settings and provide excellent readability in various lighting conditions. Both LED and LCD panels cater to the specific needs and preferences of retailers for effective digital advertising and promotion.</p></p>
