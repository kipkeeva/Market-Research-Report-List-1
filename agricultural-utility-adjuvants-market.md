<p><strong>The Agricultural Utility Adjuvants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compatibility agents</li><li>Buffers/Acidifiers</li><li>Antifoam agents</li><li>Water conditioners</li><li>Antidrift agents</li><li>Other Utility adjuvants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Agricultural utility adjuvants are substances added to agricultural sprays to improve their overall effectiveness. Compatibility agents ensure that different chemicals in the spray mix do not react negatively when combined. Buffers/acidifiers adjust the pH of the spray solution to enhance the performance of the active ingredients. Antifoam agents prevent excessive foam formation during spraying. Water conditioners improve the quality of water used in sprays by reducing impurities or hardness. Antidrift agents minimize spray drift and ensure that the intended targets receive proper coverage. Other utility adjuvants include substances with unique functions that enhance spray efficacy.</p></p>
