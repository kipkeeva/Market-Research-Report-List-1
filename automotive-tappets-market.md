<p><p>Automotive tappets are components used in engine systems to control the opening and closing of valves. The main types are flat tappets and roller tappets. Flat tappets have a flat surface that contacts the camshaft, while roller tappets have a cylindrical roller. The market for flat tappets is popular due to their cost-effectiveness and simplicity. However, roller tappets are gaining prominence due to their enhanced durability and efficiency. Both types have their own pros and cons, and their market demand depends on factors such as engine design, performance requirements, and budget considerations.</p></p>
