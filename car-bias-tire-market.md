<p><ul><li>Ordinary Bias Tires</li><li>Bias Belted Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car bias tire market consists of two main types: ordinary bias tires and bias belted tires. Ordinary bias tires have a construction where the multiple layers of cords are laid diagonally from bead to bead, providing strength and durability. These tires are more suitable for low-speed vehicles and off-road terrains. On the other hand, bias belted tires have additional belts made of steel or synthetic material that offer enhanced stability and improved tread life. These tires are commonly used in high-speed vehicles and provide a smoother ride on highways.</p></p>
