<p><p>The bow ties market can be categorized into three types: pre-tied, clip-on, and self-tie. Pre-tied bow ties come already knotted and are convenient and easy to wear, making them popular among individuals who are less skilled at tying bow ties. Clip-on bow ties feature a pre-tied knot attached to a clip at the back, providing a simpler option for those who don't want to tie their own bow. Self-tie bow ties require manual knotting, offering a traditional and sophisticated look, favored by individuals with advanced skill in tying bow ties.</p></p>
