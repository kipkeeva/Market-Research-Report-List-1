<p><p>Polyolefin hot melt adhesives are available in three different types: granular, blocky, and film. Granular polyolefin hot melt adhesives are small, spherical particles that can be easily melted and applied. Blocky polyolefin hot melt adhesives are in solid form and need to be melted for application. Film polyolefin hot melt adhesives come in the form of thin sheets that can be easily cut and adhered. Each type offers different advantages and is suitable for specific applications, providing flexibility and ease of use in various industries.</p></p>
