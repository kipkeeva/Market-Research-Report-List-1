<p><p>Car paint protective film is a popular solution to shield the paintwork from scratches and abrasions. The market offers two main types of protective films: TPU and PE. TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) films are known for their superior resilience and high elasticity, providing excellent protection against impacts, stone chips, and environmental elements. On the other hand, PE (Polyethylene) films offer a more cost-effective option, providing good resistance to scratches and UV rays. Both types serve the same purpose of preserving the car's paint finish, although TPU is often preferred for its advanced protective properties.</p></p>
