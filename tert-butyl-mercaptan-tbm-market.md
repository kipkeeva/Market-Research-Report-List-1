The market is driven by the increasing demand for TBM in the rubber industry for vulcanization, where it acts as a cross-linking agent to improve the elasticity and strength of rubber products. The growing automotive industry, especially in emerging economies, is also fueling the demand for TBM-based rubber products such as tires and hoses.</p><p>Furthermore, the pharmaceutical industry is another key driver for the TBM market. TBM is used as a pharmaceutical intermediate in the manufacture of various drugs, including cholesterol-lowering medications and anti-cancer drugs.
