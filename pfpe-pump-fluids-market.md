D-type refers to perfluoropolyether fluid, which has excellent thermal stability and chemical resistance. K-type refers to a potassium-based PFPE fluid, known for its strong oxidizer resistance. Y-type denotes a PFPE fluid with low volatility and high vacuum properties. Finally, Z-type refers to a zeolite-based PFPE fluid, characterized by its suitability for high-temperature applications. Each type has distinct properties that cater to specific industrial needs, making the PFPE Pump Fluids market diverse and adaptable.</p></p>
