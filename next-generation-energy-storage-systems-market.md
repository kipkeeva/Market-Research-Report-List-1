Batteries store energy chemically and are commonly used in electric vehicles and renewable energy systems. Fuel cells convert chemical energy into electrical energy and are often employed in transportation and stationary power applications. Flywheels store energy kinetically and are suitable for short-duration energy storage needs. Ultracapacitors store energy electrostatically, offering high power and fast charging capabilities. Energy grids facilitate the integration and management of multiple energy sources. Other technologies include thermal energy storage and hydrogen storage systems.</p></p>
