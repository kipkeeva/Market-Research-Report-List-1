<p><p>The water-filled submersible pump market consists of three types: vertical, horizontal, and inclined submersible pumps. The vertical type is designed to be placed vertically underwater, with its motor and impeller contained in a single unit. The horizontal type is placed horizontally, with the motor above water level and the impeller submerged. The inclined type is positioned at an angle underwater. These pumps are used in various applications such as agriculture, construction, and wastewater management due to their ability to efficiently pump water from submerged locations.</p></p>
