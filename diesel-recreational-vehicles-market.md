<p><p>Diesel recreational vehicles (RVs) can be broadly categorized into two types: motorized RVs and towable RVs. Motorized RVs are self-contained units that have their own engines and can be driven like a regular vehicle. They offer convenience and flexibility but are often more expensive. On the other hand, towable RVs are pulled by another vehicle, such as a truck or SUV. They are generally more affordable and allow for separate use of the towing vehicle.
