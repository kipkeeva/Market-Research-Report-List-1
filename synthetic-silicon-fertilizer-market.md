<p><p>The synthetic silicon fertilizer market can be classified into two types: liquid and solid. Liquid synthetic silicon fertilizers are in a liquid form and are typically sprayed onto plants or applied through irrigation systems. They are highly soluble and can be readily absorbed by plants, providing them with immediate nutrition. On the other hand, solid synthetic silicon fertilizers are in a granular or powdered form and are applied by broadcasting or incorporating into the soil. They release nutrients slowly over time, ensuring a continuous supply of nutrition to the plants.</p></p>
