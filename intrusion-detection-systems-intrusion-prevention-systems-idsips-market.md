<p><p>Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS) and Intrusion Prevention Systems (IPS) are security solutions designed to detect and prevent unauthorized access or malicious activities within a network or system. The rising instances of data breaches, hacking, and other cyber-attacks have led organizations across various industries to adopt IDS/IPS solutions to safeguard their sensitive information.</p><p>Technological advancements, such as the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) algorithms into IDS/IPS systems, have enhanced their capabilities in detecting and preventing sophisticated threats. The increasing integration of IDS/IPS solutions with other security systems, such as firewalls and antivirus software, is also contributing to market growth.</p><p>Overall, the IDS/IPS market is poised for steady growth in the coming years, driven by advancements in technology, increasing cyber threats, and the growing emphasis on network security by organizations across industries.</p></p>
