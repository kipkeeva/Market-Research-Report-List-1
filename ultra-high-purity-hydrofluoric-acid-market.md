UP/SEMI G4 represents HF with the highest purity level and is mainly used in the semiconductor industry. UP-S/SEMI G3 is slightly less pure, commonly used for cleaning and etching in the semiconductor and electronics sectors. UP-SS/SEMI G2 is used in various applications, including metal surface treatment. Lastly, EL/SEMI G1 indicates HF with the lowest purity, often utilized in the lighting industry.</p></p>
