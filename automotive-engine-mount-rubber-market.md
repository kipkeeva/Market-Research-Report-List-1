<p><p>The automotive engine mount rubber market is divided into three types: natural rubber, synthetic rubber, and vulcanized rubber. Natural rubber is derived from latex of rubber trees and offers excellent flexibility and resilience. Synthetic rubber is artificially made using petroleum-based hydrocarbons and exhibits high durability and resistance to extreme temperatures. Vulcanized rubber is a modified form of natural or synthetic rubber that has undergone a heating process, resulting in improved strength and hardness. These different types cater to the diverse needs of the automotive industry in terms of performance, reliability, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
