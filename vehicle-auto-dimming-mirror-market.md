<p><p>The vehicle auto dimming mirror market consists of two main types: inside auto dimming mirrors and outer auto dimming mirrors. Inside auto dimming mirrors are placed inside the vehicle, typically on the windshield or the rearview mirror, and automatically adjust their brightness to reduce glare from headlights of following vehicles. Outer auto dimming mirrors, on the other hand, are installed on the exterior of the vehicle and perform the same function by automatically dimming the mirror's brightness when exposed to bright lights, helping to enhance the driver's visibility and safety.</p></p>
