<p><p>Ballistic composites are materials used in the manufacturing of bulletproof vests, helmets, and other protective equipment. Aramid composites are made from synthetic fibers known for their high strength, resistance to cuts and heat. UHMPE composites, made from ultra-high molecular weight polyethylene, offer excellent resistance to impact while being lightweight. Glass composites provide good protection against ballistic threats but are heavier compared to aramid and UHMPE composites. "Others" refer to composites using materials like carbon fiber or ceramic. These composites offer unique properties such as high stiffness or resistance to extreme temperatures, ideal for specialized applications in the ballistic protection market.</p></p>
