<p><p>Electromagnetic suspension is a technology used in vehicles to improve their ride comfort and handling. Pure active suspension refers to a type of electromagnetic suspension system that actively adjusts the suspension to provide optimal control and damping in real-time. On the other hand, semi-active suspension utilizes electromagnetic technology to passively adjust the suspension in response to changing road conditions. While pure active suspension offers superior performance, it is more expensive than semi-active suspension, making the latter a more viable option for cost-conscious consumers.</p></p>
