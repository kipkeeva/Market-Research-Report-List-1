<p><p>Optical touch probes refer to a type of touch probe technology used in metrology applications. In the market, there are two main types: 3D touch probes and 2D touch probes. 3D touch probes enable measurement of a three-dimensional surface by capturing data points in multiple directions. On the other hand, 2D touch probes are used to measure two-dimensional surfaces, capturing point data in a single axis. Both types of touch probes employ optical technology to provide accurate and precise measurements for various applications in industries such as manufacturing and quality control.</p></p>
