<p><p>The market for 12V Truck Power Window Motors can be categorized into two types: OEM and Aftermarket. OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) motors are produced by the truck manufacturer and are sold as part of the original vehicle. These motors are specifically designed to meet the OEM specifications and quality standards. On the other hand, aftermarket motors are produced by third-party manufacturers and are available for purchase separately.
