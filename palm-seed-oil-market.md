<p><p>Palm seed oil, also known as palm kernel oil, is derived from the fruit kernels of the oil palm tree. It is also used in the cosmetic industry for its moisturizing properties and in the biofuel industry as a renewable energy source.</p><p>The global palm seed oil market has been witnessing significant growth in recent years.
