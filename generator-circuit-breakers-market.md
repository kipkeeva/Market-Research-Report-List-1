<p><p>Generator circuit breakers (GCBs) are specialized devices used to protect electric generators and power plants from overloads or faults. There are different types of GCBs available in the market, including air blast circuit breakers, vacuum circuit breakers, and SF6 circuit breakers. Air blast circuit breakers use compressed air to extinguish the arc, vacuum circuit breakers utilize a vacuum to interrupt the current, and SF6 circuit breakers employ sulfur hexafluoride gas for arc extinction. Each type has its own advantages and applications in various power generation systems.</p></p>
