<p><p>The Panoramic Radiography Flat Panel Detector market consists of two types: Silicon Crystal and Amorphous Silicon. Silicon Crystal detectors use crystalline silicon, which offers high resolution and efficient X-ray detection. Amorphous Silicon detectors, on the other hand, use non-crystalline silicon, providing high sensitivity to X-rays and better performance under low exposure conditions. Both types are widely used in panoramic radiography, offering different advantages based on specific needs such as image quality and sensitivity to radiation levels.</p></p>
