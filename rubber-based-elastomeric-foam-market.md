<p><p>The rubber-based elastomeric foam market is categorized into different types based on the type of rubber used. NBR (nitrile butadiene rubber) based elastomeric foam is known for its excellent oil resistance and is commonly used in automotive and industrial applications. EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) based elastomeric foam is highly resistant to weather, UV radiation, and extreme temperatures, making it suitable for outdoor applications. Chloroprene-based elastomeric foam offers good fire resistance and is commonly used in construction and HVAC systems. Other rubber-based elastomeric foams include those made from natural rubber or silicone rubber.</p></p>
