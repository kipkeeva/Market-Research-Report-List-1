<p><p>The needle-free drug delivery equipment market is composed of three main types: novel needle technology, inhaler technology, and transdermal patch technology. Novel needle technology eliminates the use of traditional needles by employing methods like jet injectors or micro-needles for delivering drugs. Inhaler technology, on the other hand, enables drug delivery through inhalation, targeting diseases like asthma or chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD). Lastly, transdermal patch technology allows drug absorption through the skin, providing a non-invasive and convenient way to administer medications.</p></p>
