<p><p>Snow waxes are categorized into two main market types: glide and grip. Glide waxes are primarily used for downhill skiing and snowboarding, enabling smoother sliding by reducing friction between the equipment and the snow surface. They enhance speed and maneuverability. On the other hand, grip waxes are designed for cross-country skiing and provide traction when ascending uphill. They increase grip between the ski and the snow, enhancing the skier's ability to push forward and maintain traction on steeper slopes. Glide and grip waxes cater to different snow sports, ensuring optimal performance and enjoyment for enthusiasts.</p></p>
