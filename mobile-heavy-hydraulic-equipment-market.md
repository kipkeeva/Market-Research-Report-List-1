<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Mobile Heavy Hydraulic Equipment market consists of two types of drive systems used for powering hydraulic pumps. The Direct Pump Drive system directly connects the pump to the engine or motor, allowing for a more efficient and responsive power transfer. On the other hand, the Pump-Accumulator Drive system utilizes an accumulator to store energy from the engine or motor and then releases it to the pump when needed, providing a smoother and more controlled power delivery. Both drive systems have their own advantages and are suitable for different applications in the mobile heavy hydraulic equipment industry.</p></p>
