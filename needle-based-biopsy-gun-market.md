<p><p>The needle-based biopsy gun market consists of three major types: vacuum-assisted, fine needle, and core needle. Vacuum-assisted biopsy guns are designed to extract tissue samples using negative pressure, providing accurate and complete samples. Fine needle biopsy guns use thin needles to collect cells or fluid, commonly used for diagnosing tumors. Core needle biopsy guns are used for obtaining larger tissue samples, utilizing a wider needle to extract a cylindrical core of tissue. These three types cater to different biopsy requirements and play crucial roles in diagnosing various medical conditions.</p></p>
