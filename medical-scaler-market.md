<p><p>The Medical Scaler Market encompasses two main types: Magnetostrictive Medical Scalers and Piezoelectric Medical Scalers. Magnetostrictive Medical Scalers use magnetostriction technology and produce elliptical vibrations, making them efficient in removing plaque and tartar from teeth. On the other hand, Piezoelectric Medical Scalers employ piezoelectric crystals to generate linear vibrations, enabling precise removal of calculus and stains while minimizing damage to tooth surfaces. Both market sectors offer specialized tools that aid dental professionals in dental cleaning and treatments, providing essential options tailored to their specific needs.</p></p>
