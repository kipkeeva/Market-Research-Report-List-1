<p><p>The smartphone integrated circuits (IC) market encompasses various types of chips. Dynamic random access memory (DRAM) chips store data temporarily for quick access. Micro-processor unit (MPU) chips act as the central processing unit of the smartphone, performing calculations and executing instructions. Digital signal processor (DSP) chips handle audio and video processing tasks. Application specific integrated circuit (ASIC) chips are customized for specific smartphone functions. Erasable programmable read only memory (EPROM) chips can be programmed and erased multiple times, making them suitable for firmware updates and data storage in smartphones.</p></p>
