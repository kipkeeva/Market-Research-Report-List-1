<p><p>Kraft Sealing Tape is available in two main market types: Water Activated Kraft Paper Tape and Water Free Kraft Paper Tape. Water Activated Kraft Paper Tape requires water to activate its adhesive properties during application, providing a secure and strong bond. On the other hand, Water Free Kraft Paper Tape does not need water for activation and instead relies on a pressure-sensitive adhesive. Both types offer effective sealing solutions, but their different activation methods cater to varying application and user preferences.</p></p>
