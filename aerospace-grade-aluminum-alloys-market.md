These advancements are likely to improve the performance characteristics of alloys, making them more durable and capable of withstanding extreme operating conditions.</p><p>Moreover, the market is anticipated to witness significant growth due to the increasing emphasis on sustainability and environmental friendliness in the aerospace industry.
