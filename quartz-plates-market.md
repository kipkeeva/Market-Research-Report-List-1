<p><p>Quartz plates can be classified into two types: high purity type and ordinary type. High purity quartz plates are those that have a high degree of chemical purity and minimal impurities. They are commonly used in applications such as semiconductor manufacturing and scientific research where a high level of purity is required. On the other hand, ordinary quartz plates have a lower level of purity and are used in various industries for applications such as optical instruments, electronics, and automotive.
