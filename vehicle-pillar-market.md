<p><p>The vehicle pillar market is divided into four types: A-Pillar, B-Pillar, C-Pillar, and D-Pillar. The A-Pillar is located at the front of the vehicle, near the windshield, providing structural support and containing airbags for safety. The B-Pillar, found between the front and rear doors, contributes to the vehicle's overall structural integrity. The C-Pillar is situated at the rear of the vehicle and provides support for the roof. Lastly, the D-Pillar is typically found in SUVs and serves as the rear pillar, offering stability and reinforcement.</p></p>
