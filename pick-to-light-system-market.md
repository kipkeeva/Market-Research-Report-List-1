<p><p>A Pick-to-Light system is a warehouse technology that guides the manual picking process by using light indicators. It is commonly used in order fulfillment operations where workers need to pick products from different locations within a warehouse. The system consists of modules with LED lights that are placed near each pick location. These lights are connected to a central control system, which directs the lights to illuminate when a worker needs to pick a specific item. This helps minimize errors and increase picking speed, resulting in improved efficiency and productivity.</p><p>The future outlook of the Pick-to-Light System Market appears promising. <p><p>The Pick-to-Light System market includes two main types: manual order picking and semi-automatic order picking. In manual order picking, workers rely on visual cues from lights or displays to locate and pick items in a warehouse or distribution center. This method requires human intervention at every step of the process. On the other hand, semi-automatic order picking involves a combination of human workers and automated systems. Workers receive guidance from lights or displays, but certain tasks like item retrieval may be automated.</p></p>
