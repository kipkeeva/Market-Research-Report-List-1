<p><p>The Table Top Electrical Muscle Stimulator market consists of various types. Neuromuscular Electrical Stimulation (NMES) focuses on stimulating motor nerves to promote muscle contractions. Functional Electrical Stimulation (FES) uses electrical impulses to activate paralyzed muscles. Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation (TENS) aims to relieve pain by stimulating sensory nerves. Interferential therapy applies two separate electrical currents to combat pain. Burst Mode Alternating Current (BMAC) stimulates muscles through rapid bursts of electrical impulses. Micro Current Electrical Neuro Muscular Stimulator (MENS) utilizes low-level electrical currents. Lastly, there are other types of electrical muscle stimulators available in the market.</p></p>
