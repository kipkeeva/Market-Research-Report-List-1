<p><p>The architectural paint market is comprised of various types of paints. Water-based paint is primarily made of water and is environmentally friendly, as it emits fewer volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Solvent-based paint is made of chemicals and has a strong odor due to the high levels of VOCs. Powder paint is a dry paint that is electrostatically applied and then baked to create a tough and durable finish. Other types of architectural paints include oil-based paints, acrylic paints, and specialized coatings like anti-graffiti or anti-microbial paints.</p></p>
