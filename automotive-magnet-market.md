<p><p>The automotive magnet market comprises two main types: man-made magnets and permanent magnets. Man-made magnets are created by humans using various materials and processes, such as ceramic magnets made from strontium or barium ferrite. They are widely used in automotive applications such as motors and sensors. On the other hand, permanent magnets are naturally occurring or manufactured magnets that retain their magnetic properties for a long time without any external influence. These magnets, including neodymium magnets, are commonly utilized in electric vehicles and other automotive components due to their strong magnetic fields.</p></p>
