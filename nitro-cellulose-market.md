<p><strong>The Nitro Cellulose Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flocculent</li><li>Threadiness</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nitrocellulose market can be classified into two major types: flocculent and threadiness market. In the flocculent market, nitrocellulose is sold in the form of finely powdered or granulated flakes that easily disperse in liquids. It is primarily used in the production of lacquers, paints, and inks. On the other hand, the threadiness market refers to nitrocellulose that is sold in the form of long fibers or threads. This type of nitrocellulose is commonly used in applications such as film and tape production, as well as in the manufacture of certain textiles.</p></p>
