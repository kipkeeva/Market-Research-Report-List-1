<p><p>Putty paste market is classified into different types based on their functionalities and applications. Ordinary filling putty is used for basic filling and repairing purposes. Functional putty, on the other hand, serves specific functions like thermal insulation, soundproofing, and fire resistance. Decorative putty is designed to enhance the aesthetics of surfaces by providing texture and color. Tile putty is formulated specifically for tile installation, ensuring good bonding and minimizing crack formation. These various putty types cater to different needs and are essential in various construction and renovation projects.</p></p>
