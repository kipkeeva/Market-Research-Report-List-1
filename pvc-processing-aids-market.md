MMA/acrylate copolymer aids improve PVC's melt strength, fusion, and viscosity, ensuring optimal processing. MMA/styrene copolymer aids enhance PVC's fusion, melt flow, and surface quality. The "others" category includes various aids, such as lubricants and plasticizers, that improve PVC's fluidity, stability, and overall processing efficiency. Each type of processing aid offers unique benefits for the production of PVC products.</p></p>
