<p><p>The medical temperature modulation device market consists of various types of products. Conductive patient warming systems use heat transfer through conductive materials to warm patients during surgeries or other medical procedures. Convective patient warming systems use forced air to warm patients. Conductive patient cooling systems, on the other hand, cool patients using conductive materials. Other products and accessories in this market include temperature monitoring devices, catheters, and disposable blankets.</p></p>
