<p><p>The water valve market includes different types of valves for various applications. Quarter-turn valves are designed to provide a quick and easy on/off control with a 90-degree turn, commonly used in residential plumbing systems. Multi-turn valves require multiple rotations to open or close, offering more precise water flow regulation and are often utilized in industrial settings. Control valves enable precise adjustment of water flow rates and are commonly found in complex plumbing systems, such as those used in commercial buildings or water treatment plants.</p></p>
