<p><p>Polyimide washers come in two different types: ordinary washers and reinforced washers. Ordinary washers are made of pure polyimide material without any additional reinforcements. These washers are commonly used in applications that require high temperatures and mechanical strength, such as aerospace and electronics. On the other hand, reinforced washers are made by adding reinforcing materials like glass fibers or carbon fibers to enhance their mechanical properties and increase their strength. Reinforced washers are often used in heavy-duty applications that require extra durability and resistance to wear and tear.</p></p>
