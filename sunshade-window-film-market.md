<p><p>The Sunshade Window Film market consists of three major types: Neutral Window Film, Double Reflective Window Film, and Spectral Window Film. Neutral Window Film is designed to provide a balanced heat and glare reduction without altering the appearance of windows. Double Reflective Window Film has a mirrored appearance on both the interior and exterior sides, enhancing privacy and rejecting heat. Spectral Window Film utilizes advanced technology to selectively filter and reduce specific wavelengths of light, providing enhanced energy efficiency and reducing fading on interior materials.</p></p>
