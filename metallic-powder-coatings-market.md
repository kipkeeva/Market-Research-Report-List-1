In the bonding process, metallic powder is applied to the surface using heat or adhesives, providing a strong and lasting bond. The blending process involves mixing metallic powder with a thermosetting resin, creating a homogeneous mixture that is then applied to the surface. Lastly, in the extrusion process, metallic powder is pushed through a die to create a desired shape, resulting in a textured or patterned coating.</p></p>
