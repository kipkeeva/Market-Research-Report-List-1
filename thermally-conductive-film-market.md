<p><p>The thermally conductive film market includes different types of films used for thermal management purposes. Silicone thermal conductive films are made of silicone material, which offers good thermal conductivity and electrical insulation. Aluminum film compounded thermal conductive films consist of a layer of aluminum foil combined with a thermal conductive adhesive, providing high thermal conductivity and electromagnetic shielding. Other types of thermally conductive films may include graphite films, ceramic films, and liquid metal films, each offering unique properties for various applications. These films are utilized in electronic devices, power supplies, lighting, automotive, and other industries to efficiently dissipate heat and prevent overheating.</p></p>
