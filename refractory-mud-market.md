<p><p>The refractory mud market is categorized into different types based on their composition and properties. Clay refractory mud is made from clay materials and has good thermal insulation and resistance to high temperatures. High alumina refractory mud is composed of alumina and offers excellent resistance to acids and high temperatures. Silicon refractory mud is made from silicon carbide and is known for its outstanding thermal conductivity. Magnesia refractory mud contains magnesium oxide and is valued for its high strength and resistance to alkaline substances.</p></p>
