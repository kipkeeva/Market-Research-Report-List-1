<p><p>PTFE fabric refers to a type of fabric made from polytetrafluoroethylene, a synthetic fluoropolymer of tetrafluoroethylene. There are two main types of PTFE fabric in the market - porous PTFE glass fabric and non-porous PTFE glass fabric. Porous PTFE glass fabric is a breathable fabric with tiny pores that allow for air and moisture passage, making it suitable for applications like filtration, medical dressings, and protective clothing. Non-porous PTFE glass fabric, on the other hand, has a solid surface without any pores and is commonly used in industries such as food processing, electronics, and automotive for its non-stick and heat-resistant properties.</p></p>
