Peroxides, on the other hand, act as stabilizers and improve cetane levels by reducing the potential of fuel to polymerize. Lastly, 'Others' category refers to different cetane improver compounds that may include alkyl nitrate, hydrogen peroxide, and other chemical additives that enhance the cetane number. These types play a crucial role in enhancing fuel performance and complying with environmental regulations.</p></p>
