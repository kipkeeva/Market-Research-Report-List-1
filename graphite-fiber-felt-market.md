<p><p>The graphite fiber felt market can be categorized into three types: PAN graphite felt, rayon graphite felt, and pitch graphite felt. PAN graphite felt is produced using polyacrylonitrile fibers and is known for its high thermal conductivity and excellent electrical properties. Rayon graphite felt is made from rayon fibers and offers good flexibility and thermal stability. Pitch graphite felt is derived from coal tar pitch and is characterized by high density and superior mechanical strength. These different types of graphite fiber felts are used in various industries for applications requiring high thermal insulation and conductivity.</p></p>
