It requires special infrastructure and is commonly used for public charging. </p><p>AC Charging Pile, on the other hand, utilizes alternating current and provides a slow charging option for electric vehicles. It is typically used for residential or private charging purposes. Both types play a crucial role in establishing an effective and comprehensive charging network for electric vehicles.</p></p>
