<p><ul><li>Conventional Seed Potatoes</li><li>Micro Propagation Seed Potatoes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Seed potatoes are categorized into conventional and micro propagation markets. Conventional seed potatoes involve the traditional method of growing potatoes from selected tubers, ensuring disease-free and high-quality crops. These seed potatoes are produced through natural methods. On the other hand, micro propagation seed potatoes are grown using tissue culture techniques, allowing for rapid multiplication of disease-free clones. This market type enables the production of large quantities of identical and healthy seed potatoes, suitable for commercial cultivation. Both market types aim to provide farmers with reliable sources of healthy seed potatoes for successful potato production.</p></p>
