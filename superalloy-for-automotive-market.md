<p><p>Superalloys for the automotive market are categorized into three types based on their composition: Fe-based, Ni-based, and Co-based alloys. Fe-based superalloys contain iron as the main element and offer excellent corrosion resistance and good thermal stability. Ni-based superalloys, primarily composed of nickel, provide exceptional strength and resistance to high temperatures. Co-based superalloys, mainly consisting of cobalt, possess excellent wear resistance and are highly reliable under extreme conditions. These different types cater to specific automotive applications, ensuring optimal performance and longevity.</p></p>
