<p><p>PE Pipe Market Types:</p><p>1. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) Pipe: This type of pipe is known for its high strength and durability, making it suitable for various applications such as water supply, gas distribution, and industrial uses. HDPE pipes offer excellent resistance to chemicals, corrosion, and weathering, ensuring long-lasting performance.</p><p>2. MDPE (Medium-Density Polyethylene) Pipe: MDPE pipes have a medium density compared to HDPE pipes, offering a balance between strength and flexibility. They are commonly used for natural gas distribution, irrigation systems, and underground utilities, providing reliable performance and easy installation.</p><p>3. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) Pipe: LDPE pipes have a low density, making them flexible and ideal for applications requiring bendability. These pipes are commonly used for agriculture and landscaping purposes, including irrigation systems, water transportation, and drainage solutions, due to their ease of handling and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
