<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Crop cleaning equipment market includes two types: pre-cleaning and fine cleaning. Pre-cleaning equipment is used to remove larger and heavier impurities like stones, twigs, and large foreign particles from crops. It ensures a basic level of cleaning before the fine cleaning process. Fine cleaning equipment, on the other hand, is designed to remove smaller and lighter impurities like dust, chaff, and smaller foreign particles. It provides a more thorough and refined cleaning to achieve a higher quality output. Both types are essential for optimizing crop quality and ensuring market standards are met.</p></p>
