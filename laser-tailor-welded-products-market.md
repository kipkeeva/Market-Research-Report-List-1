<p><p>Laser tailor-welded products refer to a range of market types where laser welding technology is used to create customized shapes and structures. Tailor-welded blanks involve welding together different sheet metal components to form a single piece with varying thicknesses. Tailored coils are created by welding different coils together to produce custom sizes and shapes. Tailored tubes are made by welding tubes of different diameters and/or thicknesses to meet specific requirements. Other markets include custom laser welding applications, such as combining dissimilar materials or welding non-metallic materials. These innovations offer flexibility and efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
