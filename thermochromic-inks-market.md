<p><p>The Thermochromic Inks market is composed of three main types: Cold Activated Thermochromic Inks, Touch Activated Thermochromic Inks, and Warm Activated Thermochromic Inks. Cold Activated Thermochromic Inks are designed to change color when exposed to low temperatures. Touch Activated Thermochromic Inks change color when pressure or friction is applied. Warm Activated Thermochromic Inks, on the other hand, transform their color when subjected to heat. These types of inks are widely used in a variety of applications such as packaging, security printing, promotional products, and textiles.</p></p>
