<p><p>MDI Ecological Adhesive is categorized into three types: pure MDI ecological adhesive, aggregate MDI ecological adhesive, and modified MDI ecological adhesive. </p><p>Pure MDI ecological adhesive refers to the adhesive that is made purely with MDI (Methylene Diphenyl Diisocyanate), which results in high strength and durability.</p><p>Aggregate MDI ecological adhesive is a mixture of MDI and other aggregates, such as sand or gravel, providing improved bonding properties and enhanced resistance to environmental conditions.</p><p>Modified MDI ecological adhesive is formulated by modifying the MDI chemical structure to enhance its adhesive properties, making it more suitable for specific applications or environmental conditions.</p></p>
