<p><ul><li>CNG</li><li>LNG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The natural gas for vehicle market consists of two primary types: Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) and Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG). CNG is natural gas compressed to high pressures and stored in cylinders within vehicles. It is mainly used for light-duty vehicles, such as cars and buses. On the other hand, LNG is natural gas cooled and converted into a liquid form for storage and transportation purposes. It is suitable for heavy-duty vehicles like trucks and ships due to its higher energy density. Both CNG and LNG offer cleaner and more affordable alternatives to traditional gasoline and diesel fuels.</p></p>
