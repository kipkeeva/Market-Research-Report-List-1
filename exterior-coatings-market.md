<p><ul><li>Solvent-Thinned Coatings for Exterior Wall</li><li>Solvent-Waterd Inorganic Exterior Wall</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Exterior coatings refer to the protective layers applied on exterior surfaces to enhance durability and appearance. Solvent-thinned coatings for exterior walls are coatings that contain solvents as a carrier, which evaporate during drying, leaving a hard film. These coatings are commonly used to protect exterior walls from weathering and UV rays. On the other hand, solvent-waterd inorganic exterior wall coatings are formulated using a mixture of solvents and water, and primarily made of inorganic materials. These coatings provide excellent waterproofing and weather resistance properties to exterior walls.</p></p>
