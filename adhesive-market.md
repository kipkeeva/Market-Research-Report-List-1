<p><p>The adhesive market can be classified into three types: water-based, solvent-based, and hot melt adhesives. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly and widely used in paper, packaging, and wood industries due to their low toxicity and low cost. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonding capability and are commonly utilized in construction, automotive, and electronics industries. Hot melt adhesives are solid at room temperature and become adhesive when melted, making them ideal for rapid bonding in industries like packaging and labeling. Each type has its own advantages and applications depending on the specific requirements of different sectors.</p></p>
