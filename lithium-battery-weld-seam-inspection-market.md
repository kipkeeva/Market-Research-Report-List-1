Module Inspection refers to the examination of weld seams in the battery module, ensuring their quality and integrity. On the other hand, Busbar Inspection involves inspecting the weld seams of the busbars, which are responsible for conducting electrical current in the battery pack. Both types of inspections focus on assessing the weld seam quality to ensure the overall performance, safety, and efficiency of the lithium batteries.</p></p>
