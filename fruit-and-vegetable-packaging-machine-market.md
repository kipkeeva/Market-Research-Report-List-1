<p><p>In the fruit and vegetable packaging machine market, two common types are vertical and horizontal machines. Vertical machines are designed to pack fruits and vegetables in a standing position, using gravity to fill bags or containers. They are popular for packaging delicate items like leafy greens. Horizontal machines, on the other hand, pack fruits and vegetables in a lying position, using belts or conveyors to move the produce through the packaging process. These machines are often used for bulk packaging, such as packing large quantities of carrots or apples.</p></p>
